About Anandam Restaurant

Dehri's beloved North Indian restaurant, offering unforgettable dining experiences with a warm fireplace ambiance and happy-hour food.

Where Every Meal Becomes a Celebration

Located at W52P+F93, Pali Rd, beside Mi Store, opp. HDFC Bank, Gandhi Nagar, Dehri, Bihar, Anandam Restaurant offers an exquisite North Indian dining experience with a warm fireplace ambiance, all-you-can-eat buffet, and happy-hour food — perfect for families, celebrations, and corporate gatherings.
